# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in HumanAI-Impact-Bench a respectful, harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and evidence-oriented.
- Treat lived experience and cultural context with care.
- Use content warnings for potentially distressing material.
- Protect privacy and never publish identifiable personal conversations.
- Accept constructive correction and disclose limitations.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, intimidation, discriminatory language, sexualized attention,
publishing private information, encouraging self-harm, exploiting vulnerable
people, or using project spaces for personal attacks is unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct or safety concerns through the private channel listed in
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Project maintainers may remove content or restrict
participation when behavior threatens the community or research participants.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
